The three-layer strategy

Verify (MIT01, 02, 04, 05, 07): Don't rely on name or signature alone — verify integrity, provenance, and content before loading anything. Limit (MIT03, 08, 09): Assume something will slip through — sandbox its execution, restrict what it can access, and plan for rapid isolation. Watch (MIT06): Continuously validate that what's running matches what was approved, and alert when it doesn't.
