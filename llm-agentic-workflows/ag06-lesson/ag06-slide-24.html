Reading the matrix

MIT03 (Access Controls) is the primary control for three scenarios and contributes to all six. MIT06 (Tier Separation) is the only control that fully prevents the S1 and S4 attack classes where write access is granted to a legitimate channel. S3 (cross-tenant leakage) is uniquely isolated: MIT03 alone is the primary defense — no other mitigation addresses the read-side extraction path directly.
