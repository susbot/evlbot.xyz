Slide 6 · The Outcomes
Business harm. Not server harm.
Five damage patterns from unrestricted business flow access.
🎫
Inventory Monopolization
Bots acquire all available units of a scarce resource — tickets, limited-edition products, vaccine appointments — before real buyers can. The inventory is then resold at a markup, transferring value from the business and real customers to the bot operator.
💸
Referral and Reward Fraud
Bots automate account creation and referral redemption. A single operator creates thousands of accounts, each referring the next, collecting referral bonuses at scale. The business pays out real money for fraudulent activity that generates no real customers.
⭐
Review and Rating Manipulation
Bots submit thousands of fake reviews for a business (positive) or competitor (negative). Platform integrity is undermined. Consumers make decisions based on manipulated signals. Legitimate businesses are disadvantaged.
📊
Market and Price Distortion
Bots monitor competitor pricing APIs and adjust listings hundreds of times per day in response. Price transparency tools are gamed. Automated purchasing at scale distorts supply and demand signals.
🗳️
Vote and Poll Manipulation
Bots cast thousands of votes, upvotes, or poll responses. Trending algorithms are gamed. Community signals are corrupted. Award outcomes are manipulated. Platform credibility is destroyed.
