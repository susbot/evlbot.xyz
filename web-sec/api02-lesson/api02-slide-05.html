Slide 5 · The Outcomes
What happens when authentication breaks.
Real consequences, across every industry.
🔓
Complete Account Takeover
The attacker becomes you — sees your data, acts on your behalf, locks you out. Every account-takeover breach starts with broken authentication.
💸
Financial Fraud
In-app purchases, linked bank accounts, payment methods — an attacker with your token has everything they need to spend your money. Fortnite attackers bought V-Bucks on stolen accounts and resold them.
🕵️
Mass Credential Harvesting
When login endpoints have no rate limiting, automated tools can test millions of stolen username/password pairs. One in fifty reused passwords is a taken account.
🔗
Chain Attacks
A compromised account becomes a foothold. From one user's session, attackers probe for BOLA, escalate privileges, or pivot to internal services. Broken auth is often step one of a larger attack chain.
📧
Identity Theft
Name, address, email, phone, payment history — a taken account contains everything needed to steal an identity or commit fraud in the victim's name.
